Kourtney Kardashian Shows Off Her Baby Bump in a Black Floral Mesh Dress

With Halloween fast approaching, Kourtney Kardashian is getting into character.

The Poosh founder’s latest maternity look is a mix of spooky and chic, and comes from Kardashian’s own Boohoo collection. It’s a black and white mesh maxi dress with a large graphic floral print. The piece is sleeveless and features an elegant mock neck—Kardashian’s preferred neckline. (You can shop it here.)

The reality star opted to wear the semi-sheer dress over a black lingerie set and added a dark smoky eye and a moody brown lip for an extra dose of spook.

“‘Tis the season 🎃,” she captioned a post with photos of the outfit on Instagram. In the images, she poses in what looks like her backyard, which is decorated with a pile of giant white pumpkins.

Kardashian explained in her caption that the mesh styles from her second capsule collection with online ready-to-wear brand Boohoo, including this piece, are all made from 95 percent recycled polyester.

The mom of four—who is pregnant with her and husband Travis Barker’s first child together, a boy—shared another October-core outfit post on Instagram earlier this week. That time, the post included throwback snaps from a Halloween photo shoot she had in a full Bride of Frankenstein costume (similar to the one sister Kylie Jenner wore last year), composed of a white corset minidress, black stockings, and a white fur-lined robe—plus a massive updo. “Ready for October 👰🏻‍♀️🖤,” Kardashian wrote in the caption.
