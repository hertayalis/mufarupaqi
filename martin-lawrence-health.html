Martin Lawrence Addresses Fans' Concerns About His Health: "Stop the Rumors"

"I'm in God's hands. I'm blessed. I'm glad to be waking up every day. ..."

By Joseph Allen

Jun. 10 2024, Updated 4:07 p.m. ET

Amid months of speculation about potential health concerns, actor-comedian Martin Lawrence spoke out in June 2024 to address the rumors head-on.

So what exactly is going on, and what have fans been saying about Martin's health? Here's what we know.

Why are fans concerned about Martin Lawrence's health?

In January 2024, the Emmy Awards were filled with reunions from classic TV shows. Among those reunions was one from Martin, which was Martin Lawrence's sitcom from the 1990s. The show's ensemble cast assembled to riff about the fact that they never won an Emmy, and Martin then presented the award for best actor in a comedy.

Following Martin's appearance on the show, though, many were concerned about the comedians overall well-being.

Some viewers believed that Martin had slurred his speech at times and had taken several long pauses as well, which made it seem like he was having trouble reading from the teleprompter.

"Can't be the only one concerned about Martin Lawrence," one person wrote online following his appearance on the telecast. Another person wrote: "Did Martin Lawrence have a stroke?"

Most fans expressed their concern in positive terms, saying that they hoped everything was OK with him. Others were simply grateful to see most of the cast back together again after more than 20 years off the air. "This is the reunion we needed!!! Then, in May 2024, after Martin celebrated alongside co-star Will Smith at the Los Angeles premiere of their film Bad Boys: Ride or Die, a clip from the event went viral in which Martin appears to be holding onto Will as they walk on a bus. This, coupled with the fact that Martin seems to be less energetic than his co-star, caused further concern among fans.

Martin then decided to address the rumors head-on during an interview with Hot 97's Ebro in the Morning on June 4, 2024, telling listeners, "I'm fine." He went on to say, "I'm in God's hands. I'm blessed. I'm glad to be waking up every day. I'm all good. No need for people to be concerned."

He later added: "I'm healthy as hell. Stop the rumors."

Martin has had several health incidents in the past, but those occurred largely in the 1990s and were typically connected to prolonged exposure to the heat or dehydration.

In recent decades, no news has broken to suggest that Martin is in any way unwell.
