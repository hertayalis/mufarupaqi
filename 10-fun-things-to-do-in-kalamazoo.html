10 Fun Things to Do in Kalamazoo

1. Explore the Kalamazoo Nature Center

Immerse yourself in the wonders of nature at the Kalamazoo Nature Center. This beautiful preserve spans over 1,100 acres and offers an array of outdoor activities for all ages. Take a hike along scenic trails, discover native wildlife at the nature exhibits, or enjoy a peaceful picnic surrounded by lush greenery. With its diverse ecosystems and educational programming, the Kalamazoo Nature Center is a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts.

2. Visit the Air Zoo Aviation Museum

Step into the world of aviation at the Air Zoo Aviation Museum. This interactive museum showcases aircraft from different eras, offering a fascinating glimpse into the history of flight. Explore the exhibits, participate in flight simulators, and even take a ride in a vintage aircraft. From World War II planes to space exploration memorabilia, the Air Zoo Aviation Museum has something for everyone.

3. Discover the Kalamazoo Institute of Arts

Indulge your artistic side at the Kalamazoo Institute of Arts. This renowned art museum features a diverse collection of works, including paintings, sculptures, and photography. Explore the galleries, attend a workshop or lecture, or simply admire the stunning pieces on display. With its commitment to promoting creativity and cultural appreciation, the Kalamazoo Institute of Arts is a hub for artistic inspiration.

4. Wander Through the Kalamazoo Valley Museum

Dive into the history, science, and culture of Kalamazoo at the Kalamazoo Valley Museum. This free museum offers a wide range of exhibits, from interactive displays on local industries to collections showcasing the region’s heritage. Learn about the Native American tribes that once inhabited the area, marvel at the antique cars, or delve into the wonders of the universe at the planetarium. The Kalamazoo Valley Museum provides an engaging and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

5. Stroll Along the Kalamazoo Riverwalk

Take a leisurely walk along the Kalamazoo Riverwalk and soak up the scenic beauty of the city. This picturesque pathway stretches for miles, offering stunning views of the river, parks, and downtown skyline. Enjoy a peaceful retreat in nature while still being within reach of Kalamazoo’s vibrant urban atmosphere. Whether you’re looking for a refreshing jog or a serene place to relax, the Kalamazoo Riverwalk is the perfect destination.

6. Indulge in Craft Beer Tastings

Beer enthusiasts will delight in the wide variety of craft breweries in Kalamazoo. Embark on a brewery tour and sample unique flavors at places like Bell’s Brewery, Arcadia Ales, or One Well Brewing. Immerse yourself in the local beer culture and even learn about the brewing process. With its burgeoning craft beer scene, Kalamazoo offers a delightful experience for all beer lovers.

7. Enjoy Live Performances at the Kalamazoo State Theatre

Experience the magic of live entertainment at the historic Kalamazoo State Theatre. This beautifully restored venue hosts a diverse range of performances, including concerts, theater shows, and comedy acts. Feel the energy as you watch world-class artists grace the stage, and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of this cultural landmark.

8. Explore the Gilmore Car Museum

Get a glimpse into the history of automobiles at the Gilmore Car Museum. This sprawling museum showcases a vast collection of vintage cars, ranging from classic models to rare automobiles. Take a stroll through the beautifully restored barns and admire the sleek designs of different eras. Whether you’re a car enthusiast or simply appreciate automotive history, the Gilmore Car Museum is sure to captivate you.

9. Venture to the Kalamazoo Valley Antique Market

Unearth hidden treasures at the Kalamazoo Valley Antique Market. Held monthly, this sprawling market brings together a vibrant assortment of antique vendors from around the region. Explore the stalls, discover unique collectibles, and revel in the thrill of finding that one-of-a-kind item. With its bustling atmosphere and eclectic selection, the Kalamazoo Valley Antique Market promises an exciting adventure for antique lovers.

10. Experience the Kalamazoo Mall

Stroll along the Kalamazoo Mall, a charming downtown street filled with shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues. Indulge in retail therapy at boutique stores, savor local flavors at restaurants, or catch a live music performance at one of the many pubs and bars. With its vibrant atmosphere and unique offerings, the Kalamazoo Mall is the perfect place to experience the city’s spirit and vitality.

With an array of attractions and activities, Kalamazoo offers something for everyone. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, an art lover, or simply seeking a memorable adventure, this vibrant city will not disappoint. Plan your visit today and make lasting memories in Kalamazoo.
