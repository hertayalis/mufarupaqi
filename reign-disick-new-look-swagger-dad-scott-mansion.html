Reign Disick, 8, shows off bold new look as he struts with 'swagger' down dad Scott's fancy floor in

SCOTT Disick's son Reign has shown off his changed appearance in a new photo taken inside his father's $6million mansion.

The Kardashians star took to Instagram on Wednesday to share a snap of his youngest child flaunting his swagger.

In the pic, Reign strutted across the hardwood floor sporting blue and white pants, a green T-shirt, and his newly dyed platinum blond locks.

He gave a soft smile while looking away from the camera.

Scott, 40, gushed over the eight-year-old in his caption: "Young swaggen on em."

The Flip It Like Disick alum shares Reign and his older kids: Mason, 13; Penelope, 11; with his ex, Kourtney Kardashian, 44.

Reign will soon not be the youngest in the family anymore as Kourtney is expecting her fourth child - her first with her husband, Travis Barker, 47.

The pregnant star has been showing off her growing belly in bikinis and other revealing ensembles since announcing the news in June.

She and Travis later revealed that they're having a baby boy, and they're enlisting the help of their kids to choose a name.

Travis is a father to Landon, 19; Alabama, 17; and a stepdaughter, Atiana de la Hoya, 24, with his ex-wife, Shanna Moakler, 48.

BABY NAMES

A source told Us Weekly that the couple was “having their kids contribute to the list” of potential names.

The insider said they were “including [their kids] in the baby journey whenever possible” as the baby is “a celebration for the whole family.”

Recently, Travis admitted he wanted to name his son Rocky 13 after his favorite movie and number.

The source told the outlet he was “joking when he said he was considering” the unusual name.

“Travis and Kourtney haven’t decided on a name yet, but they’re having a lot of fun coming up with different ideas,” they claimed.

STYLISH TOT

However, Travis already has some ideas for his unborn child's wardrobe, as he shared a snap of a matching outfit he had made for the father-son duo.

The Blink-182 drummer posted the pic to his Instagram Stories last month, showing off the blue jerseys with their last name plastered on the back.

While Travis' shirt appeared to have a double-digit number on the back, starting with the number two, baby Barker's was left blank.
