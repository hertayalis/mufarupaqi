Missing Matthew Listman Found Dead

On Friday night, the body of a missing man was discovered in Normal, Illinois, along a creek.

Police in Normal, Illinois, located the body of 21-year-old Libertyville resident Matthew Listman at the corner of West Summit and North Main Streets at around 10 o’clock on a Friday evening.

The image that the Normal police department released of 21-year-old Matthew Listman, who had been reported missing since Thursday and was later pronounced dead at 11:20 p.m. on Friday.

A woman from Peoria was killed in a car accident early on Saturday.
Listman drowned in a chilly environment, according to an initial autopsy report; results from a toxicological test are still pending. Authorities are looking into the cause of Listman’s death even though it does not appear to be suspicious.

Anyone with information that could assist the Normal Police Department or the McLean County Coroner’s Office is asked to call 309-454-9593 or email cso@npd.org.
