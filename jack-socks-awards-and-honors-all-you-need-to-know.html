Jack Socks Awards And Honors: All You Need To Know

Jack Sock is an American pickleball player and former professional tennis player who turned pro in 2011 and retired in August 2023 after 14 years. He achieved a career-high ranking of No. 8 in singles on November 20, 2017 and world No.2 in doubles on September 10, 2018. Over the course of his successful career, Sock won a total of 19 titles on ATP Tour; including four Grand Slams, one in the mixed doubles and three in the doubles category. Sock was ranked amongst the top-10 ATP player in 2017, having shown his class as an extraordinary talent by winning four titles in the singles category.

Did Jack Sock win a WTA Title?

Yes, Jack Sock won the WTA title on four occasions.

Did Jack Sock win a AEGON Title?

No, Jack Sock never won an AEGON title all through his career.

Did Jack Sock win ATP Title?

Jack Sock won several ATP titles during his time playing professional tennis.

Did Jack Sock win a Grand Slam?

Yes, Jack Sock won a total of four Grand Slam titles.

Did Jack Sock win ITF World Champion?

Jack Sock never won an ITF World Champion.

How many Tournaments has Jack Sock won?

Jack Sock won a total of 21 tournaments in his career. He won four career singles titles and 17 doubles titles on the ATP Tour.

How many Medals does Jack Sock have?

Jack Sock has two medals to his name. He won gold in the Mixed Doubles at the 2016 Rio de Janeiro and bronze in the Doubles also at the 2016 Rio de Janeiro.

Which year did Jack Sock win his first Grand Slam?

Jack Sock won his first Grand Slam in 2014 at the Wimbledon men’s doubles.

Has Jack Sock won any pickleball awards?

Yes, Jack Sock has won a pickleball award. He partnered with world No. 1 Anna Leigh Waters to win the mixed doubles titles in his maiden season.
