The Kardashians Season 3 Release Date, Cast And Latest News

The Kardashians season 3 is nearly here. That's right: charge your Disney+ subscriptions, because Kim, Kourtney, Kylie, Khloé, Kendall, Kris and the gang are promising a whole lot of drama in a teasy new trailer – plus they've revealed the release date of the new series.

So if you're wondering what you can expect in the next series, we've got you. Covering everything from likely plot lines to who will feature from the Kardashian Klan, here's everything you need to know about The Kardashians season 3.

What will happen in The Kardashians season 3?

The season's synopsis officially reads: “The cameras return as Kris, Kourtney, Kim, Khloé, Kendall and Kylie invite viewers back into their lives as they navigate motherhood, co-parenting, and building their own empires. Their bond will be tested and tensions arise, but as a family they will stand together through the storm.”

“I wish I could tell you this will be a mild, relaxing, serene season… It’s not,” Khloé Kardashian tells the camera in one of the opening clips of the trailer. And Kim adds: “Listen, everyone has their own truth of how they think something happened.”

It's not clear exactly which issues will be addressed in the next series of The Kardashians, but most fans can hazard a guess. We see Kim breaking down in tears in one snippet – suggesting we may see some of the fallout amidst her divorce from Kanye West – and we also see Kylie ask: “We have huge influence. What are we doing with our power?” Cryptic.

It's thought that Kim's split from Pete Davidson may also be covered, as well as Kylie finally choosing a name for her son, Aire, and potentially a look at Kourtney Kardashian and Tristan Barker's lavish third wedding in Italy, which wasn't featured in season 2.

When is The Kardashians season 3 release date?

You won't have too long until you can get your Kardashian fix, with Season 3 being released on May 25 on Disney+ for UK viewers.

Is there a trailer for The Kardashians season 3?

Yes, we were blessed with the trailer when it dropped alongside the release date! All the usual suspects appear in the new trailer, including Kourtney's ex partner Scott Disick, who declares: “I've never seen this much drama in my life.” Now that's saying something.

Who will appear in The Kardashians season 3?

We can expect all of our usual faves in the new season of The Kardashians:

  • Kris Jenner
  • Kim Kardashian
  • Khloé Kardashian
  • Kourtney Kardashian
  • Kendall Jenner
  • Kylie Jenner

Given Scott's cameo in the trailer we'll no doubt be seeing him, plus Kourtney's husband Travis Barker and momager Kris' beau Corey Gamble. Let's also not forget all of the newest additions to the family, with Kylie and Khloé's new baby boys sure to be making an appearance.

Rob Kardashian chose not to appear in the first season, with an insider telling E! News at the time “Rob is not planning to have any participation in the new Hulu show”, so it's unlikely we'll see him.
