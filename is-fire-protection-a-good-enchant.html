How good is protection enchantment?

On java, 1 piece of protection 1 protects 4%, protection 2 protects 8% and so on. A full set of protection 4 protects 16% * 4 which is 64%. Any special protection enchantments (Blast, Projectile and Fire) protects 10% at level 1 for that type of damage, all the way up to 40% at level 4.
