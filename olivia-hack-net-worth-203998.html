Olivia Hack Net Worth

Olivia Hack is an American voice actress born on June 16, 1983 in Beverly Hills, CA. She is best known for her role as the voice of Rhonda Wellington Lloyd in the Nickelodeon show Hey Arnold!. She has also appeared in other television shows and films, including The Wild Thornberrys, The Suite Life of Zack & Cody, and The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement.
