Lady Gaga Wore an Oversized Faux-Fur Coat in Italy

Lady Gaga is celebrating in Italy.

The 35-year-old singer and actress took to Instagram on Saturday to share a picture of herself enjoying the weekend. In the photo, captioned simply with two emojis—the Italian flag emoji and the celebrating person in a party hat emoji—Gaga is relaxing on a wicker chair on a balcony while dressed to maximize casual comfort and holding a fruity drink.

As usual, even Gaga's most comfiest outfits are head-turning works of art. In this photo, the House of Gucci actress wore what appears to be a matching, black and white striped legging and crop top combo under a blue plaid flannel top and an oversized red and black faux-fur coat. She accessorized the look with a pair of black boots, sunglasses, and a casual top knot.

Gaga is currently in Italy filming House of Gucci with Adam Driver. In the movie, Gaga plays Patrizia Reggiani, who was convicted for her role in the 1985 murder-for-hire of her ex-husband, Maurizio Gucci (played by Driver). The film has drawn criticism from members of the Gucci family.

"We are truly disappointed. I speak on behalf of the family," Maurizio's second cousin, Patrizia Gucci, revealed to the Associated Press. "They are stealing the identity of a family to make a profit, to increase the income of the Hollywood system....Our family has an identity, privacy. We can talk about everything. but there is a borderline that cannot be crossed."
